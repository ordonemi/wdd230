Houston Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Houston Chamber of Commerce is the name chosen for this site. This name was chosen because it accurately represents the city in which the chamber is based out of.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote the different businesses that form a part of the chamber of commerce in Houston, Texas. The site is meant to attract potential customers to the members of the organization and in turn boost the growth of these businesses. This site will also serve as a point of reference for tourists visiting Houston.
