The Space Center Houston

Space Center Houston is the official visitor center for NASA's Johnson Space Center. It offers a fascinating and interactive experience that educates visitors about space exploration, NASA's missions, and the history of the U.S. space program. Visitors can explore exhibits, astronaut encounters, and even take a tram tour to get a behind-the-scenes look at the NASA facility.

Killen's BBQ

Killen's BBQ is a Texas barbecue institution located in Pearland, a suburb of Houston. The restaurant offers a mouthwatering array of barbecue classics, including brisket, ribs, sausage, and smoked turkey, all cooked to perfection. Killen's BBQ has earned a loyal following not only in Houston but across the state and even nationally. It's a must-visit spot for barbecue enthusiasts seeking authentic Texas flavor.

Urban Harvest Farmers Market

The Urban Harvest Farmers Market is one of the largest and most popular farmers' markets in Houston. It offers a wide variety of fresh, locally grown produce, artisanal products, baked goods, and more. This market is known for promoting sustainable agriculture and supporting local farmers, making it a great place to explore and purchase high-quality, locally sourced products.
