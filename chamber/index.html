Welcome to Houston: Where Opportunity Meets Innovation

Nestled in the heart of the Lone Star State, Houston, Texas, is a city unlike any other. As the fourth-largest city in the United States, Houston boasts a dynamic blend of diverse cultures, vibrant industries, and a unique Southern charm that sets it apart. Our Houston Chamber of Commerce is proud to be your gateway to this thriving metropolis, providing you with the resources, connections, and support you need to thrive in our community.

The Space Center Houston

Space Center Houston is the official visitor center for NASA's Johnson Space Center. It offers a fascinating and interactive experience that educates visitors about space exploration, NASA's missions, and the history of the U.S. space program. Visitors can explore exhibits, astronaut encounters, and even take a tram tour to get a behind-the-scenes look at the NASA facility. It's a hub for space enthusiasts and a vital part of Houston's identity as Space City, USA.

Killen's BBQ

Killen's BBQ is a Texas barbecue institution located in Pearland, a suburb of Houston. Pitmaster Ronnie Killen is famous for his dedication to perfecting the art of smoked meats. The restaurant offers a mouthwatering array of barbecue classics, including brisket, ribs, sausage, and smoked turkey, all cooked to perfection. The meat is known for its rich flavor and tender, smoky goodness. Killen's BBQ has earned a loyal following not only in Houston but across the state and even nationally. It's a must-visit spot for barbecue enthusiasts seeking authentic Texas flavor.

Urban Harvest Farmers Market

The Urban Harvest Farmers Market is one of the largest and most popular farmers' markets in Houston. It offers a wide variety of fresh, locally grown produce, artisanal products, baked goods, and more. You can find everything from fresh fruits and vegetables to handmade crafts and prepared foods. This market is known for promoting sustainable agriculture and supporting local farmers, making it a great place to explore and purchase high-quality, locally sourced products. The market often hosts special events, cooking demonstrations, and educational activities as well.
