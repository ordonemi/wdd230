Santa Monica

Known for its iconic pier, Santa Monica is a vibrant coastal city with a famous beach, bustling boardwalk, and a lively downtown area. The Santa Monica Pier features an amusement park, aquarium, and various shops and restaurants. The beach offers opportunities for swimming, surfing, and beach volleyball.

Laguna Beach

Renowned for its stunning natural beauty, Laguna Beach is a picturesque coastal town nestled among cliffs and coves. It's famous for its crystal-clear waters, tide pools, and pristine sandy beaches. Art galleries, boutiques, and gourmet dining establishments line the streets in the downtown area.

La Jolla

Located in San Diego, La Jolla boasts some of the most breathtaking coastline in California. Its rugged cliffs, sandy beaches, and abundant marine life make it a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. La Jolla Cove is known for its crystal-clear waters ideal for snorkeling and kayaking, while the nearby Children's Pool Beach often hosts harbor seals and sea lions.
